# Linear Algebra

Linear algebra is the language of modern science. It is what lets us describe
geometry, dynamical systems, statistics, machine learning, quantum mechanics,
computer graphics, and signal processing in a single shared vocabulary of
vectors and matrices.

By the end of this short course you'll understand:

- what vectors and vector spaces are, and why they matter
- how linear maps and matrices act on vectors
- how to decompose matrices via eigenvalues and eigenvectors

This is a beginner-friendly course. You do not need any college math
background; high-school algebra is enough.
